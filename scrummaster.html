We are looking for a SCRUM MASTER EXPERIENCED for one of my client
Objectives of the mission
The Regulatory Expertise Center of the COO of my client is a transverse entity at the service of the Domains and Businesses which aims to guarantee the application of the best Compliance standards (Compliance, Legal, Risk). Within the Center of Expertise, the KYC program pilots projects aimed at improving customer knowledge (KYC) in the Retail/Private Banking business lines and its integration into the customer and employee processes. The program ensures cross-functional coordination of the various project stakeholders: business lines (general secretariat, change management, business development, marketing), IT, operations and compliance. The Scrum Master will have to propose the most efficient strategies possible to support two Program Squads while keeping the customer experience at the heart of his concerns.
He/she will have to support the Squads to move forward in an autonomous way, to set up practices, rituals and collaborative tools (backlog management, visual management). He/she will ensure continuous improvement and above all the appropriation of the Agile mindset.
Scrum Master activity
- Implementation and animation of Agile rituals (requirements definition, product backlog, sprint planning, review, feedback...)
- Animation of different project bodies
- Experience in project management in SCRUM/KANBAN mode
- Coaching on methodology and facilitation .
