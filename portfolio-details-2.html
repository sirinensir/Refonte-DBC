Project description

Our project aims at solving the different problems related to the opening of locks. Whether it's a broken, blocked or lost key, our goal is to offer a simple solution. The proposed solution is to develop a mobile application for locksmith assistance for the company ND Menuiserie with a Dashboard administrator for monitoring statistics.
